we work to create a culture that is safe and comfortable for people of all genders. Our purpose is to open up a conversation around gender with honesty and trust to shift culture to one of inclusion and acceptance.

our signature program includes:


vision session

meet with company leaders to discuss current company culture and desired outcome from this partnership. we develop a customized value-driven plan based on your company needs and values. we work in partnership - this means we want to hear from you what changes you want to see and what are the current problems.


1:1's with employees

engage in dialogue with employees in a safe and confidential manner. provide advice and coaching around navigating gender-related conflict in the workplace. this helps us understand what is going on in the company through various perspectives and angles.


group workshops

facilitate activities, discussion, and reflection through various group forms (by team, role, gender, etc). workshops are tailored based on strategy and are interactive and participant focused


strategy and progress sessions

sustained channel of communication with leadership to open communication, share learnings, and provide assessments. we discuss feedback on partnership and the progress the company is making.


handbook guide

curriculum for each employee with teachings from gender theory, philosophy, psychology and nature to guide our learning. handbook includes a guidebook that provides questions for personal reflection to digest learnings.

partnership with us is a mutual relationship: you commit to investing in a sustained value-driven plan, we commit to meaningful and impactful change in your company culture.
