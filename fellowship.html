becoming allies


we are excited to launch our first fellowship for men in tech on feminism.

this fellowship is designed for men in tech to learn about feminism and how to become allies for diversity and inclusion. we will come together to learn and process notions of masculinity and uncover unconscious bias. we will provide teachings from gender theory, philosophy, psychology and nature to guide our learning.

fellowship includes 4 workshops taking place on wedensday evening in downtown sf:

1. deconstructing gender: introduction to gender theory and getting to know the systems we were born into

wednesday january 18th, 2017

2. understanding language: how we communicate and how we harm each other with our words

wednesday january 25th, 2017

3. emotional vulnerability: how to cultivate empathy and share our feelings

wednesday february 1st, 2017

4. mindfulness transformation: where do we go from here and how to continue this process

wednesday february 8th, 2017


each participant receives pivot to bloom handbook and workbook for personal reflection and continued learning


cost: $350

open to anyone who identifies as man in tech
